Silver Lake Campground East

Silver Lake Campground East is located 52 miles east of Jackson on Highway 88 at an elevation of 7,200 feet in the Eldorado National Forest.

CAUTION: There are bears in the area. Please keep all foods, soaps, and garbage properly stored in bear boxes.

Check in time is 2:00 p.m. Check out time is 1:00 p.m.

Interagency Senior and Access passes are accepted for single family units. Give your Interagency pass number when making a reservation.

Reservations are available through Recreation.gov for many sites. Day use fees apply for park entry.

Maximum stay limit is 14 days per district per year.

Campfires may not be allowed during fire restrictions. Please use the grill or fire ring provided.
