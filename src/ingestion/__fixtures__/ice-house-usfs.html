Ice House Campground

Ice House Campground is located 23 miles east of Placerville on Highway 50 then 11 miles north on Ice House Road then 1.5 miles east on Forest Road 32.

Check in time 1 pm, check out 11 am. Camping on the boat ramp is permissible in self contained vehicles only, no campfires allowed at boat ramp.

The campground is generally open between Memorial Day Weekend and mid-October. These dates are affected by snow conditions.

Single site fee is $36. Double site fee is $72. Interagency Senior and Access passes are accepted for single family units.

Parking allowed only in designated camping spurs and parking areas.

Bear boxes are provided at campsites. Food must be stored properly to prevent bear encounters.

Reservations are handled through Recreation.gov for many sites at Ice House Campground.
