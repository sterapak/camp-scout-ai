Silver Lake and Silver Lake West Campground

Silver Lake is located just off Highway 88 at an elevation of 7,200 feet in Amador County and is part of EID's Hydroelectric Project 184 system licensed with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC).

There is a boat launch, day-use areas, and restrooms. EID also operates a Silver Lake West Campground located adjacent to the lake.

Currently there is no water service at Silver Lake West Campground. Please plan accordingly.

Silver Lake provides an excellent destination for high alpine recreation activities. Surrounded by the Eldorado National Forest this is an exceptional location for picnicking, hiking, fishing, and boating.

Camping at Silver Lake West

Silver Lake West Campground is first come, first served. Reservations are not accepted.

Camping fees include one vehicle. Additional vehicle fees may apply where parking is limited.

Seasonal alert: High winds may affect boat launch operations during winter storms.

Quiet hours are enforced from 10 PM to 6 AM.
