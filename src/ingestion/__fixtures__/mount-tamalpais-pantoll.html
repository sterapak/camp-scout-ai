Pantoll Campground

Pantoll Campground is located in Mount Tamalpais State Park at about 2,000 feet elevation. The small campground offers forested sites with access to ridge trails and coastal views.

Amenities include restrooms, picnic tables, and fire rings. Sites are recommended for tent camping.

Rules and Regulations

Pantoll Campground is first-come first-served only. Quiet hours are enforced from 10 PM to 6 AM. Dogs are allowed on leash in the campground and on fire roads.

Fires are permitted only in provided fire rings. Maximum of eight people per site.

Reservation Information

Reservations are not accepted at Pantoll Campground. Day use fees apply for park entry. Check-in begins at 2 PM and check-out is by noon.

Alerts

Seasonal alert: High winds may close ridge trails during winter storms. Check the park kiosk for updates.
